# HeptaCore Product Brief

HeptaCore is a multi-tenant AI marketing operating system for businesses that need continuous RRSS execution without losing strategic control.

## Current Product Stage

HeptaCore is an MVP tecnico pre-produccion. The product has a deployable Next.js console, tenant-aware data model, Auth.js credentials login, approvals, reports, readiness checks, dry-run worker code, and Turpial as the first seeded tenant example.

It is not yet cleared for real social publishing. Production use still requires environment configuration, database migrations, seed execution, worker infrastructure, and explicit publishing approval.

## Promise

Convert market, audience, product, competitor, content, interaction, and operational signals into actionable marketing decisions: strategy, content, campaigns, responses, leads, approvals, and reports.

## Target User

- Agencies managing several clients.
- Operators who need a stronger alternative to traditional community management.
- Founders with products that need structured social growth.
- Service businesses that need a content, response, and reporting engine.

## Differentiators

- Strategy first, content second.
- Tenant-isolated workspaces and approvals.
- Agent council for product, growth, RRSS, security, data, UX, and compliance review.
- Human approval gates for sensitive actions.
- Campaign overhead model: 35% over real platform spend, approved before execution.

## MVP Positioning

HeptaCore does not promise guaranteed sales or unsupervised automation from day one. It provides a disciplined operating layer that improves consistency, follow-up, technical judgment, approvals, dry-run execution, and reporting.
